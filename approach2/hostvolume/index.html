We roast in the same room we cup in.

Meridian started as a single drum roaster in a garage in 2016. It's grown into a proper roastery, but the habit hasn't changed: every batch gets tasted before it's bagged, and if it doesn't hit the profile we're aiming for, it doesn't ship.

We work with the same three growing partners year over year, paying above the Fair Trade minimum regardless of the commodity market, because a roaster is only as good as the farms it depends on.
